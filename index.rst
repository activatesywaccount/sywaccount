Overview
--------

You can activate your SYW Mastercard online, through the Citi Mobile App, or by phone.  
The steps below are simple, secure, and beginner-friendly.


Before You Start
----------------

Have the following ready:

- Your SYW Mastercard (card number, expiration date, CVV)
- Primary cardholder verification details (such as last 4 digits of SSN)
- Your Citi/SYW username and password (or access to your registered email/phone for verification codes)


Option 1: Activate Online (Fastest)
-----------------------------------

#. Go to `activate.syw.accountonline.com <https://activate.syw.accountonline.com>`_.
#. Enter your card number, name on card, CVV, and last 4 digits of SSN.
#. Complete the identity verification steps.
#. If you are new, create your Citi/SYW online account.
#. You will see a confirmation once your card is successfully activated.


Option 2: Use the Citi Mobile App
---------------------------------

#. Open the **Citi Mobile App** and sign in.
#. Select your SYW Mastercard.
#. Tap **Activate** or **Finish Setup**.
#. Follow the on-screen verification steps.
#. If asked, confirm or create your card PIN.


Option 3: Activate by Phone
---------------------------

- Call the activation number printed on the sticker on your SYW card.
- Follow the automated instructions.
- Provide your card details and SSN verification when prompted.
- Your card will activate once verification is complete.

.. tip::
   Remove the activation sticker after finishing so your card details remain fully visible.



Troubleshooting
---------------

- **Card not appearing in your account:** Log out and sign in again, or verify that you're using the correct Citi profile.
- **Verification codes not received:** Check spam/junk folders, ensure your phone number/email is correct, then resend the code.
- **Details not matching:** Ensure your name, SSN, and card information match what Citi has on file.
- **Activation page not loading:** Clear browser cache, disable VPN, or use a different browser such as Chrome or Edge.
- **Still unable to activate:** Call the number on the back of your SYW Mastercard or use the help section in the Citi Mobile App.
